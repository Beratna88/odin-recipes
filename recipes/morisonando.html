Morisoñando

Description

Morir soñando (To die dreaming) is a popular beverage of the Dominican Republic which has made its way to other Caribbean and Latin American countries, usually made of orange juice, milk, cane sugar, and chopped ice.

Ingredients

  • Oranges, lemons or passionfruits
  • Whole milk or evaporated milk
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • Vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. Juice the oranges, lemons or passionfruits making about 1½ cups of juice.
  2. Add the ice to a pitcher. Pour the evaporated milk, sugar, and vanilla over the ice and stir well to combine
  3. Add the juice and stir until combined and slightly frothy.
  4. Serve in a glass with ice.
