Mangú

Description

Mangú is the Dominican Republic's national breakfast. This traditional Dominican dish can also be served for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Green Plantains
  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Peel and boil the plantains for 15 minutes. Add Salt to the water.
  2. Sauté the onions.
  3. Smash the boiled plantains with butter and milk.
  4. Garnish with the sauté onions.
  5. Serve with fried salami, eggs or cheese (or all three).
